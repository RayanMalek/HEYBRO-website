02 — Signature Graphic Language

The Checkerboard

The checkerboard is the brand's most recognizable visual asset. It wraps every post, story, and menu as thick border bands — always flanked by a solid orange or red stripe. It's not a background, it's a frame that says "this is Hey Bro."
